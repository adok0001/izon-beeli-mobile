# Beeli — Monetization Model

*Distilled from `docs/marketing-strategy.md` §8. Paired with the `beeli-finance`
skill (the P&L / runway view) and `beeli-skill/references/business-model.md`.*

## Guiding principle

**The moat is community scale. Monetization must never gate features that drive
community growth.** Language content, gamification, community, and cultural
content stay permanently free. Revenue rides on top. Test for every idea: *does
it ride on top of the free product, or tax the flywheel?* If it taxes the
flywheel, it's wrong.

## Tiers (priority order)

### Tier 1 — Free (core, permanent)

All lessons/audio/transcripts, all gamification (XP, streaks, leaderboards, daily
challenges, multiplayer), all community (feed, contributions, bounties), all
cultural content (Adinkra, Ge'ez, proverbs, dictionary), spaced-repetition review.
This tier is the product, the marketing engine, and the moat. Sacred.

### Tier 2 — Beeli Plus (individual)

**$4.99/month or $39.99/year.** Convenience on top of free:
- Auto-restocking streak freezes (free earns freezes via XP; Plus auto-stocks)
- Offline audio downloads
- Advanced word-review scheduling
- Profile customization (badge colours, accent themes)
- Ad-free (if ads ever introduced to free)
- Early access to new language content

**Hard gate:** do not launch until **D60 retention > 15%**. Premature paywalls
kill free-to-premium conversion. The 90-day plan soft-launches a "Support Beeli"
CTA to Level 5 "Scholar"+ users first. Target conversion once live: **>2% of MAU.**

### Tier 3 — Beeli Educator (institutional) — first real revenue

The product is already built (classroom, group management, invite-code
enrollment, assignments, progress tracking, content panel). This is a positioning
+ landing-page + billing activation, near-100% margin.

| Plan | Monthly | Students |
|---|---|---|
| Classroom Starter | $99 | up to 30 |
| Classroom Pro | $199 | up to 100 |
| Institution | Custom | Unlimited |

**GTM:** heritage language schools first (shorter cycle), then universities.
**90-day target:** 5+ accounts, $500+ MRR.

### Tier 4 — Sponsored Language Grants

Governments, diaspora foundations, and cultural orgs fund a bounty pool for
endangered/underdeveloped languages; Beeli runs the contribution workflow and a
progress dashboard. Mission revenue + press + content that strengthens the free
product — the strongest-aligned line. Model as project grants, not recurring.

## Unit-economics notes

- **CAC target ~zero** for primary channels; paid acquisition deferred until D30
  retention is measured.
- **The contribution economy is a content-cost lever** — each bounty is content
  Beeli didn't pay an editorial team to produce. Content cost falls as community grows.
- **Educator tier is near-100% margin** — marginal cost is support time.

## Sequencing

1. Educator tier (revenue now; product exists)
2. Beeli Plus (after the D60>15% gate)
3. Sponsored Grants (relationship-driven; press unlocks it)

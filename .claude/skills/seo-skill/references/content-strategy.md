# Beeli — Content Strategy & Blog Plan

Blog content is the fastest way to rank for informational keywords about
Izon and African languages, capture "People Also Ask" boxes, earn backlinks
from diaspora and linguistics communities, and build the authority to compete
for broader language learning terms.

Target: 2 posts/month minimum. Each post = 800–1,500 words.

---

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Izon Language Education (Informational)
The highest-priority pillar. Zero competitors own this territory. Converts
curious diaspora members and language learners into active users. Also earns
backlinks from linguistics blogs, Nigerian cultural organisations, and universities.

### Pillar 2: African Heritage Language Learning (Informational → Commercial)
Positions Beeli as THE platform for diaspora language learning — broader than
Izon alone. Targets users searching for African language apps who haven't
heard of us yet.

### Pillar 3: Culture, Identity & the Diaspora (Brand Building)
Language is inseparable from identity. Content about Niger Delta culture,
diaspora experience, and the importance of preservation builds emotional
connection, drives shares within Nigerian and Izon communities, and earns
natural backlinks.

### Pillar 4: Educator & Institutional Resources (B2B / Institutional)
Teachers, cultural organisations, and universities are high-value audiences.
Content targeting them drives educator sign-ups, institutional partnerships,
and authoritative backlinks.

## Post Structure Template

Every blog post must follow this structure for maximum SEO impact:

```
[H1] — Contains primary keyword, under 65 chars

[Introduction — 100–150 words]
Hook → state the problem or question → preview the answer.
Mention the primary keyword naturally in the first 100 words.

[H2] — [Sub-topic 1]
[H2] — [Sub-topic 2]
[H2] — [Sub-topic 3]

[H2] How to Learn [Topic] with Beeli
→ This is where we introduce Beeli. Link to the relevant feature page.
→ Include: "Start learning Izon for free at izon-beeli.com"

[H2] Frequently Asked Questions
→ 3–5 FAQ items targeting "People Also Ask" queries
→ Add FAQPage schema markup

[Conclusion — 100 words]
Summary + CTA ("Start your first Izon lesson — free, no download required")
```

## Content Publishing Workflow

1. **Plan:** Pick topic. Confirm target keyword via keywords.md.
2. **Brief:** Outline using the template. Identify People Also Ask targets.
3. **Write:** 800–1,500 words. One H1, multiple H2s. FAQ section required.
4. **Optimise:** Title tag, meta description, image alt text, internal links.
5. **Schema:** Add FAQPage schema. Add Article schema with datePublished.
6. **Publish:** Ensure it appears in sitemap.ts. Submit URL to Search Console.
7. **Distribute:** Share to diaspora community groups, educator networks, social.
8. **Link:** Add internal links from 1–2 existing pages to the new post.

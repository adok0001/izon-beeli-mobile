# Beeli — SEO Skill

You are implementing SEO for Beeli (also known as Aurufie), an African
language learning platform focused on Izon and other Niger Delta languages.
The goal is to own every search query related to Izon language learning
globally, rank for broader African/heritage language learning terms, and
drive app installs and active learners — eliminating dependence on paid
acquisition as the primary discovery channel.

**Domain:** izon-beeli.com  
**Web stack:** Next.js 15 App Router · TailwindCSS · Clerk auth · PostHog  
**App:** React Native/Expo SDK 54 (iOS + Android)  
**Languages supported:** English, French, Nigerian Pidgin (pcm)

## The One-Paragraph Strategy

Beeli is currently the only app in the world for learning Izon — a language
spoken by over 2 million people with zero presence on Duolingo, Babbel, or
any major platform. The fastest path to dominance is: **own every Izon-
specific search term globally** (competition is near-zero today), expand into
broader Niger Delta and African heritage language searches, and build the
authority to rank for "learn African language" and "language preservation app"
over 6–12 months. App Store visibility and diaspora community presence are
equally critical — most users will discover Beeli through community channels
and app store search, not Google alone. Every lesson page, dictionary entry,
and blog post is a durable SEO asset. Treat them that way.

## Non-Negotiable SEO Rules

Apply these to every task without being asked:

1. **Every page has exactly one H1** — contains the primary keyword for that page
2. **Title tags follow the format:** `[Primary Keyword] | Beeli`
3. **Meta descriptions are 150–160 characters** — include keyword + value prop
4. **All images have descriptive alt text** — "izon-language-lesson-beeli-app.jpg"
5. **URLs are lowercase, hyphenated, keyword-rich** — `/learn/izon` not `/learn?lang=iz`
6. **Internal links use descriptive anchor text** — never "click here" or "learn more"
7. **Schema markup on every public page** — SoftwareApplication, EducationalOrganization, Course, FAQPage where applicable
8. **Page speed < 2.5s LCP on mobile** — next/image for all media, lazy load below fold
9. **Multi-language metadata** — title/description in en, fr, and pcm where relevant
10. **Every new public page gets submitted** to Google Search Console within 24 hours of going live
